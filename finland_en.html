Story

I got to Finland with an ERASMUS scholarship, and during my time there I've learnt a lot about Finns and Skandinavia, though I still regret I didn't do a nice trip around the peninsula. Below you can find a couple of pictures and happenings about my time up there - unfortunately I didn't have a camera, so all the pictures are published here due to the kind support of my friends.
