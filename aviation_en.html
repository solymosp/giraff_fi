Where it all began...

...I couldn't really tell. I always liked aviation, and then during my university years I came up with the idea of finding a permanent hobby for myself which I would enjoy with time until I don't know when. So I went to the Esztergom airport to have a lookaround, and get some information on gliding - it didn't seem much trouble, so I jumped in. This was autumn 2002, since that time I have completed my second level training, now I'm going on with duration flights towards the official gliding licence.

On this page you can find a couple of photos about aviation life in Esztergom, the way I see things through the eyes of my brother's digicam (I should buy an own one soon, being without is the webmaster's handycap) If you want to see and know more, visit the AeroClub Esztergom website!
