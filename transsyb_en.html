Story

At the end of my ERASMUS studies in Tampere, Finland, with a friend of mine I decided to take a trip to Russia. I had been planning this trip for a while at that time, and I imagined it in an unorganized fashion without tourist agencies and fix time schedules - it turned out that in Miha I just found the perfect partner for these kinds of adventures.

We made up our minds to take the transsyberian train to the lake Baykal and once we were there it seemed to be logical to go over to Mongolia if we can get a visa without difficulties. So it was, and after one month tripping I flew back from Ulanbaatar to Helsinki while Miha continued his trip toward China and spent further 25 days on the go. On this page I'd like to give an image of our marvellous trip with photos, extracts from our tripping log, hope you'll have a good time reading them!
